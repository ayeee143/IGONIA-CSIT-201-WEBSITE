The Psychology of Style


Our clothing not only reflect our identity but also influence our thoughts, feelings, and behavior. Psychologists explore “enclothed cognition,” revealing how our attire impacts our self-perception and mental processes. Understanding this psychology empowers us to use fashion to boost confidence, uplift mood, and authentically express ourselves.

Inclusive Fashion: Celebrating Diversity


Fashion welcomes all, regardless of age, size, gender or background. Here, we honor the rise of inclusive fashion, embracing diversity and advocating for representation. From inclusive sizing to gender-neutral clothing lines, we explore how fashion brands and designers are breaking down barriers and championing inclusivity in all its forms.
